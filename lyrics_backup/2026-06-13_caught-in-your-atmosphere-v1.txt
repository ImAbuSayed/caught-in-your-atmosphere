[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the heat of your skin pulling me near
Losing my breath when you whisper in my ear
Tangled up in the sheets as the shadows disappear
I'm beautifully caught in your atmosphere

[Verse 1]
We parked the truck far away down a quiet dirt road
Leaving the headlights off, watching the night unfold
Your fingers trace my collar in the dashboard glow
Moving so close, taking everything nice and slow

[Pre-Chorus]
The world outside is fading to a silent blur
I'm hanging on the edge of your every whisper
Tasting the midnight rain on your lips
Feeling the electric pull of our fingertips

[Chorus]
'Cause you're the gravity holding my restless soul
A sudden rush taking over my control
I breathe you in, and the distance disappears
Forever caught in your atmosphere

[Post-Chorus]
Falling, falling, falling deep
A memory my heart is bound to keep

[Verse 2]
